About Us

The Waste Management team is tasked with developing a Smart Waste Management System
to streamline waste collection, recycling, and environmental monitoring. The system will cater
to three primary user roles: Household Users, Waste Collection Services, and Administrators.
Household Users can manage their waste collection schedules and track recycling efforts.
Waste Collection Services will use the system to optimize routes and monitor collection efficiency.
Administrators will oversee the entire system, analyzing environmental impact metrics and ensuring
effective waste management practices. The goal is to enhance sustainability and efficiency in waste
management through smart technology.
